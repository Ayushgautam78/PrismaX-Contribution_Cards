How It Works
①
Answer 9 quick questions about your PrismaX journey: your role, activity, and how long you've been part of the community.
②
Your score is calculated based on your answers. Posts, points, attendance, role, and tenure all count toward your final score out of 100.
③
Your card is generated: a unique FIFA-style operator identity card matching your tier. Higher tiers unlock rarer card designs.
④
Download or share your card directly to X with a pre-filled tweet. Show your fellow operators where you rank.
Card tiers are based on your PrismaX Discord role:
Reactive Assistive Proactive Exploratory Stabilized Navigational Groundbreaker ✦
Your answers are never stored or shared. Everything runs in your browser.
